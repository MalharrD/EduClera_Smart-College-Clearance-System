## ğŸ“š For Department Staff

### Your Dashboard
After logging in, you'll see:
- **Pending Requests**: Number of requests awaiting your approval
- **Approved**: Requests you've approved
- **Rejected**: Requests you've rejected
- **Total Processed**: All requests you've handled

### Pending Requests Page
1. Click **"Pending Requests"** in the navigation
2. You'll see a list of students waiting for your approval
3. For each request, you can:
   - View student details
   - See clearance type (Hall Ticket or No-Dues)
   - Click **"Approve"** or **"Reject"**
   - Add remarks (required for rejection)

### Workflow Rules

#### Hall Ticket Clearance
- Sequential workflow: Teachers â†’ HOD
- You can only approve when it's your turn

#### No-Dues Clearance
**Independent Departments** (can approve anytime):
- Library
- Hostel/Bus
- TPO
- Exam Cell

**Sequential Departments** (must wait for previous):
1. Student Section (first)
2. Scholarship (after Student Section)
3. Accounts (after Scholarship)

## â“ Common Questions

### Q: I'm a staff member but the main login doesn't work
**A:** The main login is for students only. Click **"Staff Login"** button and follow the staff access process.

### Q: What is the staff access password?
**A:** The staff access password is `Phoenix@123` (case-sensitive).

### Q: I can't approve a request
**A:** Check if:
- The request is in your pending list
- Previous departments have approved (for sequential workflows)
- You have the correct permissions

### Q: How do I see all students as HOD?
**A:** Login as HOD, go to your dashboard, and click the **"All Students"** tab.

### Q: Can I approve requests from other departments?
**A:** Only HOD and Admin have cross-department access. Other staff can only approve for their department.

### Q: Where do I download my clearance certificate?
**A:** Students can download from the **Track Status** page after all approvals are complete.
